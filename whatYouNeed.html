We understand that some of you haven’t done anything like this before, so we have created a checklist of things you will need to join our adventure.

Essentials:

Backpack 60-70L size
PLB Emergency Beacon
Head torch
Water
Sleeping mat
Sleeping bag Note temperature: comfort -3 degrees (minimum)
Tent
Fire lighter
First aid kit
Your personal medicine
Panadol/Voltaren/Ibuprofen
Lunch for two days(e.g. wrap with parmesan cheese and salami)
Snacks for two days (e.g. muesli bar, chocolate, lollies, nuts, dried fruit)
Dinner (Backcountry freeze-dried meal - it’s always good to carry one extra meal)
Breakfast (Oats, milk powder, cinnamon, brown sugar, tea/coffee)
Pocket knife
Compass
Topo map
Toilet paper
Baby wipes
Sunscreen
Lipbalm with SPF
Toothbrush + toothpaste
Pack liner or pack rain cover
Cutlery
Plate/bowl
Mug

Clothing layers:

Hiking shoes
Socks + spare pair (recommend proper hiking socks to be more comfortable)
Merino base layer (long sleeve top and pants)
Fleece
Puffer jacket
Rain jacket
Hat/beanie
Gloves
Shorts/hiking pants
T-shirt
Jandals

Optionals:

Walking poles
Travel pillow
Hand sanitiser
Insect repellent
Spare plastic bag to pack your rubbish
Sleeping bag liner
Small microfibre towel

Stove, gas and pots are also essential items, but since we are going together as a group, we can arrange between us a few people to carry these and we can share it with the group.
